What is AsK4Car?

Askari Bank Limited is one of the Best Car financing banks in Pakistan.Ask4Car is Askari Bank's conventional auto financing solution, which can be used to finance new, used and imported vehicles. Askari Ijarah Bis Sayyarah is Askari Bank's Islamic car leasing solution for new, used and imported vehicles.Askari Bank Car Loan product offers a simple financing solution, allowing customers to purchase their own vehicle while benefiting from a wide range of features. This provides an affordable option that is simple to apply for and quick at the same time.

Finance Policy :

  • Minimum doccuments and quick processing.
  • Tenure of Loan is from1 to 7 years.
  • Minimum Age for Borrower should be 21 years.
  • Maximum Age till Maturity of Loan is 62 years for Salaried and 65 years for SEB/SEP/Pensioner.
  • Minimum Salary/Income of borrower: Permanent Employees; Rs. 25,000/- Contractual Employees; Rs. 40,000/- Pensioners; Rs. 50,000/-
  • SEB/SEP branch customers 40000 Average Balance - SEB/SEP non branch customers 65000 Average Balance.
  • Finance Amount (subject to DBR) Rs. 200,000/- to Rs. 20 Million.
  • (Mark up rate)Branch Customer: 1 Year KIBOR + 4% - Non-Branch Customer: 1 Year KIBOR + 5%.
  • Imported: In case of imported vehicles, the bank shall not finance the vehicles older than 5 years.
