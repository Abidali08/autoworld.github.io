Faysal Islamic Car Finance is based on the principles of Diminishing Musharakah. This is a medium and long term financing product, where the Bank and the customer jointly purchase an asset and create joint ownership in the asset. The Bank rent out its share in the asset to the customer while the customer periodically purchases units of ownership in the asset from the Bank. After purchase of all the units, the customer becomes the sole owner of the asset. This feature is applicable on new cars only.Faysal Car Finance PaySmart feature provides customer ease and convenience with easy on your pocket installment plans

  • PaySmart is an option under which customer can pay lower installments in initial years as compared to standard EMI option.

  • Rs. 50/- per day will be levied on late payments after the due date. These charges are calculated and payable at the time of maturity/settlement of facility.
  • The customer is required to pay at least 15% of vehicle value and down payment varies on case to case basis.
  • Facility can be terminated at Customer’s request on payment of 5% penalty on principal outstanding amount.

  • In case of customer having a negative credit history and if he applies for new loan, the bank may not accept or approve the application of the customer.
