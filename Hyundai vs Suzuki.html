THE RACE FOR NUMBER ONE

Maruti Suzuki and Hyundai have been ruling the Indian Auto Sector since long. Both auto giants offer a myriad of choices ranging from economical hatchbacks, compact sedans, SUVs and many more. Choosing between them can be considered as one of the hardest jobs for a new car buyer. Let’s take you through a brief comparison between the two, just so to help you if you are planning to buy a new car.Maruti Suzuki is commonly known as India’s own brand. Although, It’s 56% subsidiary is owned by parent company Japan’s Suzuki Motor Corporation. Their merger was declared in the year 1982, a year after the formation of Maruti Suzuki. It’s the first plant was established at Manesar and employees Nearly 40,000 people.Maruti is 100% desi brand over as Hyundai is from South Korea. Some of the models (Hyundai) price is higher than Maruti Suzuki. Now coming to features what they are offering, Maruti never beat to Hyundai.
