What is Alfalah Roshan Apni Car?

Bank Alfalah listed in as one of the best car financing banks in Pakistan with low interests.Alfalah Islamic Auto Finance is simply a rental agreement under which the car is given to the customer on rent for a period agreed at the time of the contract. The customer is required to deposit an initial amount (security deposit) with the Bank. Upon completion of the lease period the customer has two options either to return the car and take away the security deposit or take ownership of the car against his security deposit or any other agreed amount via separate sale transaction.

Eligibility Criteria :

  • Must be Bank Alfalah Roshan Digital Account Holder.
  • Min 15% of Vehicle’s Value Up to Rs.6.0M Financing & Min 20% of Vehicle’s Value for Financing above Rs. 6.0M
  • 1 – 7 Years for all vehicles
  • 100% lien shall be marked equivalent to finance amount on Islamic Roshan Digital Account or Islamic Naya Pakistan Certificate till the maturity of Facility.
  • Minimum balance in Islamic Roshan Digital Account or investment in Islamic Naya Pakistan Certificate should be equivalent to finance amount.
  • Minimum Age 21 Years to 65 Years at time of Facility maturity FOR SALARIED PERSON
  • Currently employed, with a total employment experience of 06 months FOR SALARIED PERSON.
  • U$D 3,000/- per month or equivalent FOR SALARIED PERSON.
  • Minimum Age 21 to 70 years at time of Facility maturity FOR SELF EMPLOYED PERSON.
  • U$D 4,000/- per month or equivalent FOR SELF EMPLOYED PERSON.
  • Minimum 1 Years in current Business FOR SELF EMPLOYED PERSON.
